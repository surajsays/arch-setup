✅ What changed

    hw.gpu.mode=swiftshader_indirect → stable software rendering

    snapshot.present=false → no broken save states

    fastboot.forceColdBoot=yes → always fresh boot

    fastboot.forceFastBoot=no → disables snapshot fast boot
